BLOG: COVID PROJECT: CHICKENS!

I've been playing around with the idea of getting chickens for some years now, and the COVID lockdown seemed the perfect opportunity to take the project on.

About a year in I'm happy to report, I LOVE having chickens.

We had very specific site requirement, so I knew it was going to have to be a custom build. I'd built things in the past, but this was definitively the most ambitious woodworking project I had ever taken on. I had the support of my husband when I needed it, but I took ownership of the design and build.

It turns out power tools are fun, and I love working outside, so this was a win-win for me.
